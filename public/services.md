## Shingle Roofing

Asphalt shingle roofing is the most popular roofing choice in Florida. Midas Roofing installs architectural 30-year and 50-year shingles using Owens Corning materials. Shingle roofs offer a balance of cost, durability, and aesthetics ideal for Florida's climate.

**Best for**: Residential homes, budget-conscious replacements, re-roofs  
**Lifespan**: 25–50 years depending on product  
**Material**: Owens Corning Duration, TruDefinition, and Oakridge series

---

## Metal Roofing

Standing seam metal roofing offers unmatched longevity and performance in Florida's heat and hurricane conditions. Metal roofs reflect solar heat, reducing cooling costs, and can withstand 140+ mph winds.

**Best for**: Long-term investment, energy efficiency, coastal/hurricane-prone areas  
**Lifespan**: 40–70 years  
**Styles**: Standing seam, corrugated metal panel, metal shingles

---

## Tile Roofing

Concrete and clay tile roofing is a hallmark of Florida and Mediterranean architecture. Tile roofs provide superior wind resistance (150+ mph rating), natural ventilation, and are highly resistant to mold, rot, and insects.

**Best for**: Premium homes, Mediterranean/Spanish architecture, coastal properties  
**Lifespan**: 50+ years  
**Material**: Concrete tile, clay tile, flat tile, barrel tile

---

## TPO Roofing

Thermoplastic Polyolefin (TPO) is a single-ply membrane used for flat and low-slope roofs. TPO is highly reflective (white membrane), energy-efficient, and resistant to UV, chemicals, and punctures.

**Best for**: Commercial buildings, flat residential roofs, low-slope additions  
**Lifespan**: 15–30 years  
**Applications**: Retail, office, warehouse, residential additions

---

## Roof Coatings

Elastomeric and reflective roof coatings extend the life of existing roofs by 10–15 years. Coatings seal minor cracks and seams, reflect UV radiation, and improve energy efficiency without a full tear-off.

**Best for**: Aging flat roofs, commercial buildings, budget restoration  
**Types**: Silicone, acrylic elastomeric, polyurea  
**Benefit**: Avoid full replacement cost
