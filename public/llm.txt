## Company Overview

Midas Roofing & Construction is a licensed roofing contractor based in Longwood, Florida, serving the greater Orlando metro and all of Central Florida. Founded over 20 years ago, the company is led by CEO Jenson Perazada. The company holds Florida Roofing License CCC1334831 and is fully insured. Midas Roofing is an Owens Corning Preferred Contractor.

## Services

Midas Roofing & Construction provides the following roofing services:

- **Shingle Roofing**: Installation and replacement of architectural and 3-tab asphalt shingles. Owens Corning materials used. Best for budget-conscious homeowners seeking durability.
- **Metal Roofing**: Standing seam and metal panel systems for maximum longevity (40–70 years). Ideal for Florida's climate.
- **Tile Roofing**: Concrete and clay tile roofing — premium aesthetic with excellent wind resistance for Florida homes.
- **TPO Roofing**: Thermoplastic polyolefin flat roofing for commercial and residential low-slope applications.
- **Roof Coatings**: Reflective and elastomeric coatings to extend existing roof life and improve energy efficiency.
- **Commercial Roofing**: Large-scale flat and low-slope roofing systems for commercial buildings.
